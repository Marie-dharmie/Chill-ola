Frequently Asked Questions

  • Check-in: Rooms are available from 2:00 pm on the day of arrival.
  • Check-out: Kindly check out by 11:30 am on your departure day.
  • Cancellation Policy: Bookings may be canceled free of charge up to 96 hours before arrival.
  • Environmental Care: We are committed to sustainability—recycling, energy-efficient appliances, and mindful water use. Please help us conserve resources by reusing towels when possible.
  • Laundry Service: Laundry is available on request with a 48-hour turnaround. Please contact reception for details and pricing.
  • Reception: Our team is available 24/7. Should you need an extra blanket or pillow, or have any requests, we are always happy to assist.
  • Safety: Please keep valuables secured, do not leave personal belongings unattended in public areas, and retain your room key at all times.
  • Non-Smoking Policy: Chill Ola is a smoke-free facility. A ₦1,000,000 fee applies for violations, in addition to legal proceedings as necessary.
